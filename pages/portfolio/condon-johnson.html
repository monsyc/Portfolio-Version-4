CONDON JOHNSON'S WEBSITE
  • Date:
    Work in progress
  • Type:
    Web Desing, UI, UX, Branding
DESCRIPTION

Born in the heart of the West Coast, Condon Johnson is a construction company with 30+ years of experience in the field. Surprisingly, this was also the time they had kept their old image! The company was in need of an extreme makeover - and boy, did we make it. Our team updated their whole corporate branding, from the page layout to the logo, ‘cause you know what they say - go big or go home. We strived for a fresh and smart look, while maintaining a user-friendly layout. The outcome was a polished, intuitive website that can be easily used by anyone, ages 0 to 99. For this project, I fully worked on the website’s design, as well as the interactions, and developed the flowchart, site maps, wireframes, mock ups and prototypes. In addition, I also developed the redlining files to be handed over to the developers so that they could prepare the page’s code. The branding and collateral design, however, were made by graphic designers - because even superheroes need help sometimes ;)
