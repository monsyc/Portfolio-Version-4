DESCRIPTION

If there’s something to say about millennials, it’s two things: even in the rush of their daily routines, they actually make an effort to care for their health, and they are all tech enthusiasts. Green is the new black and nerd is the new sexy! This was the inspo behind Juicy, the mobile app. But not just any mobile app. Juicy offers users a quick reference on the benefits of fruits and vegetables, how to juice them and what kinds go best together. It also includes a description of different juicers available to help users determine which one suits them best. In terms of design, I kept the layout as I like my juice: natural and uncomplicated. Relying solely on repeating colors and fonts, vectors for consistency, and illustration, Juicy brings to the table a simple yet effective interface to bring youngsters back on the healthy track. In addition to the mobile app, I prepared the wireframes, site map, flowcharts, mock ups and a prototype in InvisionApp to show how the app works.
